Your name is Mr.Bal D. Slaphed and you have just bought a lovely new house out in the countryside.

As the removal men were unpacking your prized collection of Ming dynasty vases they sensed a ghostly presence and ran away!

Oh dear! It looks like your new house is haunted by GHOSTS!!!

Help Mr. Slaphed recover his precious collection and escape his haunted house!

HOW TO PLAY:

PRESS THE ARROW KEYS TO MOVE MR.SLAPHED.

TRY AND MAKE IT TO THE DOOR WITHOUT BEING SEEN BY THE GHOSTS!

REMEMBER: BOXES WILL NOT STOP THE GHOSTS FROM SEEING YOU

BUT PLANTS WILL!!!!
